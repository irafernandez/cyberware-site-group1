Common Scam Warning Signs

Urgent Demands

Red Flag: "Act now or your account will be closed!"

Legitimate organizations don't create false urgency. Take time to verify.

Money Requests

Red Flag: "Send money immediately to avoid consequences."

Real companies don't demand instant payments via gift cards or wire transfers.

Personal Info Requests

Red Flag: "Verify your Social Security number for security."

Legitimate organizations already have your information and won't ask via unsolicited calls.

Too Good to be True

Red Flag: "You've won $10,000! Just pay processing fees."

Legitimate prizes don't require upfront payments or fees.

Caller ID Spoofing

Red Flag: Number looks local but caller claims to be from a bank.

Scammers can fake caller ID. Always verify independently.

Robocalls

Red Flag: Automated message about your car warranty or legal troubles.

Hang up immediately. Legitimate businesses use human representatives.

How to Protect Yourself

Don't Answer Unknown Numbers

Let unknown calls go to voicemail. Legitimate callers will leave a message.

Verify Independently

Look up the organization's official number and call them back directly.

Block Suspicious Numbers

Use your phone's blocking feature and report spam numbers to your carrier.

Ask Family & Friends

Consult trusted people before sharing information or sending money.
